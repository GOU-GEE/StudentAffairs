## 📖 Introduction
The **Student Affairs Management System** is a comprehensive, one-stop platform designed to streamline and digitize college student management. Built with a modern tech stack (Spring Boot 3 + Vue 3), it provides a highly efficient and interactive experience for both students and administrators. 

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. 🧑‍🎓 Comprehensive Student Profile Management (SPA)
A centralized, Single-Page Application (SPA) view for managing complete student life cycles.
* **Basic Info:** Name, Student ID, Portrait Photo, Contact, ID Number, College, Major, Grade/Class, Campus, and Dormitory.
* **Detailed Background:** Off-campus residential address, medical history, hobbies, personality traits, and career goals.
* **Connections:** Head teacher contact info, family background (parents' contacts), and emergency contacts (minimum of 2).
* **Special Status:** Financial aid qualification levels (including upload portals for supporting documents).
* **Self-Service & Security:** Information update request workflows (e.g., address/contact changes) and strict export permission control (limited to administrators and the individual student).

### 2. 💰 Scholarship & Financial Aid Services
Full-process management for financial support workflows.
* Online application and multi-level approval for scholarships and grants.
* Publishing of work-study job opportunities.
* Student application submission and automated eligibility screening.

### 3. 📈 Academic Development Support
Tracking and managing students' academic and extracurricular achievements.
* Inquiries for academic grades, honors, and disciplinary records.
* Submission of mid-term evaluations.
* Generation of Extracurricular Activities (Second-Classroom) transcripts.
* Entry and tracking of competition awards.
* **Admin Tool:** Centralized batch data import processing for high-efficiency management.

### 4. 🎯 Career Planning & Development
Full-lifecycle support for student career paths.
* Built-in career assessment and evaluation tools.
* Appointment scheduling system for professional career counseling.
* Targeted push notifications for relevant employment and internship information.

### 5. 🛡️ Safety Management & Early Warning
Ensuring student safety and campus security.
* Registration for late dormitory returns due to emergencies.
* Out-of-campus leave reporting and approval workflows.
* Safety knowledge learning modules and mandatory online exams.
* Real-time reporting system for anomalies (e.g., campus safety hazards, personal emergencies).

### 6. 💬 Communication & Interaction Hub
Bridging the gap between students and faculty.
* 1-on-1 private messaging between head teachers and students.
* Publishing of class and grade-level announcements.
* Interactive feedback and suggestion message board.
* Dedicated zones for academic exchanges and event registrations.
